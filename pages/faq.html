Frequently Asked Questions

Ordering

How do I place an order?
Browse our shop, add items to your bag, and proceed to checkout. We accept Visa, Mastercard, AmEx, PayPal, and Apple Pay.

Can I modify or cancel my order?
Please contact us within 2 hours of placing your order at hello@fetchlab.co and we will do our best to accommodate changes.

Shipping

How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping takes 7–14 business days. Express shipping takes 3–5 business days. See our Shipping page for full details.

Do you ship worldwide?
Yes! We ship to over 45 countries. Free shipping on orders over $120.

Returns

What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day return policy on all unused items in original packaging. See our Returns page for details.

How do I start a return?
Email hello@fetchlab.co with your order number and we will send you a return label.

Products

How do I find the right size?
Check our Size Guide for detailed measurements for each product.

Are your products safe for dogs?
Absolutely. Every product is vet-approved and tested for safety and comfort.

Size Guide

How to Measure Your Dog

For the best fit, measure your dog while they are standing in a relaxed position.

Neck: Measure around the base of the neck where the collar sits. Add 1–2cm for comfort.

Chest: Measure around the widest part of the ribcage, just behind the front legs.

Back Length: Measure from the base of the neck to the base of the tail.

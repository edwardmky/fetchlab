Shipping & Returns

Shipping

We ship worldwide from our Hong Kong warehouse.

Standard Shipping (7–14 business days): FREE on orders over $120. $12.99 for orders under $120.

Express Shipping (3–5 business days): $18.00 flat rate worldwide.

Priority Overnight (1–2 business days): $35.00 — available to US, UK, and select countries.

All orders are dispatched within 1–2 business days. You will receive a tracking number via email once your order ships.

Returns & Exchanges

We want you (and your dog) to be completely happy with your purchase.

  • Returns accepted within 30 days of delivery
  • Items must be unused and in original packaging
  • Return shipping is free for UK and US customers
  • Refunds processed within 5–7 business days

To start a return, email hello@fetchlab.co with your order number.

Customs & Duties

International orders may be subject to local customs duties and taxes. These charges are the responsibility of the recipient and vary by country.
